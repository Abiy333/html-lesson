HAIRCARE TIPS TAILORED TO YOUR HAIR TYPE

CARING FOR STRAIGHT HAIR

Use lightweight products: Lightweight shampoos, conditioners, and styling lotions hydrate straight hair without weighing it down, keeping it smooth and sleek.

Turn down the heat: You might be tempted to dial up the heat when styling your straight hair, but this isn’t the answer to more volume or movement – in fact, it’s more detrimental. By taking the Temperature Calculator and using your ideal styling heat, your hair’s health and strength will soon shine through.

Invest in a scalp massager: Massaging your scalp helps to revitalise the skin and support thicker looking hair when incorporated into your daily routine, supporting visibly stronger, fuller hair.

TIPS FOR MANAGING WAVY AND CURLY HAIR

If you have curly hair, you’ll know that no hair day is the same. These top tips will help you keep your curls bouncy, frizz-free, and well-defined:

Hydrate and define: Use a deep conditioning treatment like our Magical Remedy to keep your curls soft and defined (especially for tighter curl patterns like 3c and 4c).

Control the frizz: After washing, ‘plop’ your curls with a microfibre towel or t-shirt, or scrunch gently with a normal towel. Before blow-drying or air-drying, apply a specially formulated curl product like a gel or cream to keep frizz at bay.

Use a diffuser: Use the Cloud Nine Airshot Pro hair dryer to diffuse your hair evenly for bouncy, defined curls.
